George Winston's 1982 tribute to the winter season, "December", has sold more than 3 million copies and been certified triple-Platinum by the RIAA. December is the fourth solo piano album from George Winston. It was recorded during the fall of 1982 and was released at the end of the year. It is a Christmas album, and more generally a tribute to the winter season. The album is a follow-up to Winter into Spring from earlier in 1982. December was inspired by the early winter season, and was the follow-up to George's solo piano recordings, Autumn and Winter Into Spring.
